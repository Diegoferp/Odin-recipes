Oven Baked Lamb Chops

This oven-baked lamb chops recipe is a simple and delicious way to prepare lamb. The chops are seasoned with herbs and spices, then baked to perfection in the oven. Ideal for a special occasion or a hearty weeknight dinner.

Ingredients

  • 4 lamb chops
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon fresh rosemary, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon fresh thyme, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • 1 lemon, sliced (for garnish)

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. In a small bowl, mix the olive oil, minced garlic, chopped rosemary, chopped thyme, paprika, salt, and black pepper.
  3. Rub the lamb chops with the seasoning mixture, making sure they are well-coated on both sides.
  4. Place the lamb chops on a baking sheet or in a baking dish.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven for 15-20 minutes, or until the internal temperature reaches 145°F (63°C) for medium-rare or 160°F (71°C) for medium.
  6. Remove the lamb chops from the oven and let them rest for 5 minutes before serving.
  7. Garnish with lemon slices and serve.
